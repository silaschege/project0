Precolonial Colonial Postcolonial

Precolonial period

  • In 3rd Millennium BC this is when first Kenyans settle the land
  • 500 BC-1000 AD
    1. This when Bantu Groups settle in Southern Kenya
    2. Cushites settle into Kenyan coast
    3. Arabians and Indians stettle in the Kenyan coast
    4. Southern Nilotic speaking communities move south
  • In 8th Century AD the Swahili langauge develops as an East African lingua franca
  • In 1498 April Vasco da Gama reaches coastal Kenya
  • In 1505 Portugal begin rule presence in Mombasa
  • In 1593 Fort Jesus is built in Mombasa to solidify Portuguese hegemony
  • In 1698 Omani Arabs capture Fort Jesus
  • In 1830 Slave trade flourishes
  • In 1846 25 August First Christian mission is founded by Johann Ludwig Krapf

Colonial Kenya

  • In 1850 Africa is being mapped by Europeans
  • 1895
    1. United Kingdom takes over East Africa
    2. The East African Protectorate begins
  • In 1900 Koitalel Arap Samoei predicts railway completion
  • In 1901 George V is Kenya's new mornarch
  • In 1903 The Ugandan Railway is completed
  • In 1905 Nairobi is BEAC's new capital
  • In 1909 Witchcraft is illegal in East Africa
  • In 1914 Kenya is one of British military bases for World War I
  • 1920
    1. BEAC is transformed to Kenya Colony & Uganda Colony
    2. Young Kikuyu Association (renamed the East African Association) started by Harry Thuku.
    3. Kikuyu Central Association formed
    4. Sir Edward Northey becomes the first Kenyan ruler (governor)
  • In 1944 Kenya African Study Union is founded by Harry Thuku
  • In 1947 Jomo Kenyatta is new president of Kenya African Union
  • In 1952
    1. Multiracial pattern of quotas allowed
    2. Mau Mau Uprising begins
  • In 1953 8 April Kenyatta is jailed
  • 1962
    1. KANU-KADU coalition government is formed
    2. Mau Mau Uprising ends
  • 1963 12 December
    1. Kenya Receives Independence
    2. Kenya transforms into a commonwealth
    3. Jomo Kenyatta becomes Kenya's first Prime Minister serving Elizabeth II
    4. Malcolm MacDonald becomes Kenya's last Colonial governor
  • 1964
    1. Kenya transforms into a republic
    2. Jomo Kenyatta becomes President of Kenya

Post Colonial Kenya

  • In 1967
    1. East African Community formed
    2. Kenyan pro-divisions founded
  • 1969 Tom Mboya assassinated
  • In 1973
    1. The Lokiriama Peace Accord is signed by Turkana people and Matheniki of Uganda
    2. Lufthansa Flight 540 crashes
  • In 1974 Re-election occurred
  • in 1977 Rhamu incident occurs
  • In 1978
    1. Jomo Kenyatta dies
    2. Daniel Arap Moi becomes new Kenyan President
  • In 1980 Garissa massacre takes place
  • In 1982
    1. Kenya becomes a one party state
    2. The General Service Unit is formed
  • In 1984 Wagala Massacre occurs committed by Kenyan troops
  • In 1987 Kenya hosts All African Games
  • In 1991 Parliament repeals one party section of the constitution
  • In 2002
    1. 62% rejects KANU's presidential candidate
    2. Moi becomes longest served president of Kenya's history
  • 2007 Kenya Violence Begins
  • In 2009 the UN Secretary general Kofi Annan handed names of the main suspects of the 2007 Post-election violence to the International Criminal Court
  • In 2012
    1. First LGBT pride event held in Kenyan US embassy
    2. George Saitoti and Orwa Ojode are killed in a deadly helicopter crash
  • In 2013 Kenyan General Elections are held where Uhuru Kenyatta won the election
  • In 2015-2016
    1. A Huruma building collapses, where over 100 are affected
    2. Garissa University attacked by gunmen, killing 140 and wounding 65
  • In 2017 Kenyan General Elections are held but annulment occurs,then proceeds in 26 October
